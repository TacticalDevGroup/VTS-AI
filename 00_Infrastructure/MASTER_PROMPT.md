## 6. Core Business Challenges

**Priority 1: Post-migration organic recovery.** The January 2025 Shopify to BigCommerce migration caused significant traffic and revenue loss. No rigorous post-mortem has been done. The internal team that executed the migration is still at Viktos and their account may be defensive or incomplete. This is the fastest credibility path for TDG: scope the damage, identify root causes, show early value before the retainer is a month old.

**Priority 2: Brand identity and persona alignment.** Viktos has 206K Instagram followers with no uniform audience definition and unclear buyer personas. "Cool guy gun bro" is Nick's read, but this has not been formally defined. The conversion gap is partly a brand strategy problem, not a social media problem. Identity clarity must precede channel optimization.

**Priority 3: Commercial channel build.** B2B dealer outreach, buy group support, rep group evaluation, and B2B dealer portal (BigCommerce B2B + NetSuite integration) are all in progress or needed. Evans Group was discussed as a short-term rep option; no decision made.

**Priority 4: Attribution.** No current attribution capability. This is a core TDG differentiator and should be positioned early.
