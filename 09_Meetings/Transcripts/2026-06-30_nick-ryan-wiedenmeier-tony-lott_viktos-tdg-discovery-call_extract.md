6. **Klaviyo in place but email performance not fully evaluated.** Ryan confirmed Klaviyo is their email platform. Nick asked to see automations, conversion rates, and messaging structure.
7. **Tag Manager tracking may be inaccurate.** PPC bids potentially running against bad data. No direct confirmation but Nick flagged it as a first-stop diagnostic.
8. **Dealer acquisition underperforming.** Dealer inquiry form exists but conversion from inquiry to stocking dealer has been poor. Sportsman's Warehouse and Shields both interested but 2027 before implementation if started now.
